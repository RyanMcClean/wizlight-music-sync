Frequently Asked Questions



What is Bulb Bop?

Bulb bop is a web application designed to manage and control wiz smart bulbs and sync them up with your music.

How do I install the Bulb Bop?

You can install the Bulb Bop by following the instructions provided in the installation guide on our website, https://github.com/RyanMcClean/wizlight-music-sync


Technical Questions

What technologies are used in this project?

This project uses Django (python) for the backend and HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for the frontend.

How do I connect my smart bulbs to the application?

You can connect your smart bulbs to the application by clicking on the "Find Bulbs" button and allowing the application to search for bulbs on your network


Troubleshooting

What should I do if my bulbs are not responding?

If your bulbs are not responding, try restarting the application and ensuring that your bulbs are properly connected to the network.

How do I connect bulbs to my network?

WizBulbs are connected to your internet via the WizApp, information about the app and links to download it can be found here: https://www.wizconnected.com/en-us/explore-wiz/app

Who can I contact for support?

If you need further assistance, please contact our support team at support@bulbbop.com.
